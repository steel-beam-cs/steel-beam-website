# Steel Beam Website Master Specification

## Project

Steel Beam Contractor Solutions LLC is a contractor-focused financial advisory firm providing bookkeeping, payroll support, job costing, contractor startup guidance, reporting, and practical business support.

This specification is the source of truth for the public website build.

## Fixed Assets

- Official header/banner logo: `public/images/steel-beam-banner.png`
- Official full logo: `public/images/steel-beam-logo.png`
- Founder portrait: `public/images/founder-adam-davis.png`
- These assets must be placed as image files. Do not redraw, regenerate, reinterpret, or modify the logo.

## Brand

- Brand statement: Helping Contractors Build Stronger Businesses.
- Slogan: Solid Solutions, Reinforcing Your Business.
- Internal principle: Built on Integrity. Reinforced by Trust.
- Voice: professional, trustworthy, approachable, construction-savvy, educational, modern, and steady.
- Design philosophy: confidence through clarity.
- Visual balance: 75% professional financial firm, 25% construction industry.

## Palette

- Structural Steel Gray: `#4B5563`
- Construction Orange: `#D97706`
- Blueprint Blue: `#1D4ED8`
- Concrete White: `#F9FAFB`
- Safety Yellow: `#FACC15`, limited use
- Charcoal Black: `#111827`
- Steel Navy: `#071827`

## Typography Direction

- Headings: strong modern sans-serif direction, approved as Montserrat-style.
- Body: clean readable sans-serif direction, approved as Source Sans Pro-style.
- Accent quotes: restrained serif/italic direction, approved as Merriweather-style.

## Site Architecture

1. Home
2. About Steel Beam
3. How We Help
4. The Steel Beam Client Journey
5. Contractor Success Center
6. Secure Client Portal
7. Answers Before You Need Them
8. Let's Start the Conversation

## Core Pages

### Home

Purpose: build trust quickly and direct the right contractor to schedule a consultation.

Required sections:
- Hero with logo/navigation and primary CTA.
- "Which best describes you?" guided cards.
- "Meet Your Construction Financial Partner."
- Service overview.
- Client Journey preview.
- Contractor Success Center preview.
- Closing CTA.

### About Steel Beam

Purpose: explain why Steel Beam is trustworthy.

Key themes:
- Army service, mathematics background, tower construction, project management, construction oversight, CFO/owner experience.
- $10M+ construction company experience.
- Lessons from a major customer bankruptcy and need for diversification.
- Integrity, respect, stewardship, excellence, partnership.

### How We Help

Purpose: present services by outcomes, not generic accounting categories.

Services:
- Monthly bookkeeping and financial organization.
- Payroll support.
- Job costing and contractor reporting.
- Contractor Business Foundation service.
- Catch-up and cleanup bookkeeping.
- QuickBooks setup and systems improvement.
- Business advisory and Blueprint Reviews.

### Client Journey

Phases:
1. Discovery
2. Foundation
3. Framework
4. Reinforcement
5. Blueprint Review
6. Partnership

Purpose: show the structured process and reduce uncertainty.

### Contractor Success Center

Purpose: become a useful resource center, not just a marketing page.

Include:
- Knowledge Center
- Blueprint Library
- Calculators
- Planning Center
- Blueprint Brief newsletter concept
- Contractor Success Stories future area

### Secure Client Portal

Purpose: reassure clients that document handling is professional and secure.

Include:
- ShareFile URL and CTA.
- What to upload.
- First-time user guide.
- Security promise.
- Support email and phone.

### Answers Before You Need Them

Purpose: answer common pre-client and client questions.

Include:
- Getting started.
- Working together.
- Services.
- Security.
- Pricing.
- Growth situations.
- Ask Adam CTA.

### Let's Start the Conversation

Purpose: single website-wide consultation workflow.

Form content:
- Contact info.
- Business type.
- Years in business.
- Employee count.
- Services needed, including Contractor Business Foundation.
- Current bookkeeper.
- Current accounting software.
- Preferred day/time.
- How did you hear about us.
- Blueprint Brief opt-in.
- What would success look like six months from now?

## Website Standard

Every page must make clear within three seconds:

1. Who Steel Beam helps.
2. What Steel Beam does.
3. What the visitor should do next.

Every page should leave visitors more informed, more confident, and more respected.
