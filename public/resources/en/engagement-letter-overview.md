# Engagement Letter Overview

This overview helps clients understand the working relationship before services begin.

Steel Beam Contractor Solutions LLC provides contractor-focused bookkeeping, payroll coordination, job costing, reporting, cleanup guidance, and advisory support based on the agreed scope.

Clients remain responsible for timely access, accurate source documents, business decisions, tax filings unless separately arranged, and review of reports.

Steel Beam handles sensitive business information with care and uses secure document collection whenever practical.

Final scope, pricing, timing, and responsibilities should be confirmed in the signed engagement letter.
