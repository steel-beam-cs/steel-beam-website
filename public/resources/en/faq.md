# Steel Beam FAQ

## Do you work only with contractors?

Steel Beam is contractor-focused and built around construction business needs.

## Is payroll included?

Payroll is offered as a standalone service and can be added when needed.

## Do you provide job costing?

Yes. Job costing support can track labor, materials, subcontractors, overhead, and project margin.

## Do you support Spanish-speaking contractors?

Yes. Steel Beam provides Spanish-language website pages, documents, and language assistance whenever available. Steel Beam does not advertise as a fully bilingual office.

## Are prices fixed?

Published prices are starting points. Complex cleanup, advisory, or job costing work may require a custom quote.
